What Michel Offers

Culinary leadership grounded in precision, structure, and operational reality

Michel’s consulting work is designed for hospitality operations that require more than cosmetic change. The focus is on building kitchens that perform with clarity, discipline, and consistency, while protecting the integrity of the culinary identity behind them.

Whether supporting a pre-opening, refining an established concept, or restructuring a kitchen under pressure, each engagement is approached with the same objective: to create systems, standards, and team alignment that hold up in real service.

Refined culinary direction supported by disciplined systems, strong brigade standards, and operational clarity.
