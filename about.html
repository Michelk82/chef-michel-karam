Biography

A Career Built on Precision, Leadership, and Standards

Michel Karam is an Executive Chef and culinary consultant with more than 25 years of continuous professional experience across luxury hotels, resorts, diplomatic institutions, and multi-outlet hospitality operations.

His career has taken him through Lebanon, the Gulf, Europe-linked hospitality environments, and Asia, where he has led kitchens, developed concepts, supported pre-openings, and built operational systems designed for consistency, quality, and long-term performance.

Today, his work combines culinary excellence with disciplined kitchen structure, helping hospitality brands strengthen execution, improve efficiency, and create guest experiences that feel elevated without losing control behind the scenes.

Calm. Disciplined. Authoritative. Professional.

Culinary Philosophy

Where Creativity Meets System

Michel’s philosophy is built on three principles: discipline, respect, and consistency. Great cuisine begins with ingredients and technique, but it becomes sustainable through organization, communication, and a kitchen culture shaped by standards.

His approach is clear: creativity matters, but it must be supported by strong systems. The best kitchens do not rely on chaos or last-minute improvisation. They operate with structure, accountability, and calm control, even under pressure.

This philosophy shapes every consulting engagement, from menu development and workflow design to brigade training and operational performance.
